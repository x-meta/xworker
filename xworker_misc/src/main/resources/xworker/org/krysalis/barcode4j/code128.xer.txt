<h2>Notes</h2>

<div class="section">
<ul>
	<li>The height should be at least 0.15 times the barcode&#39;s length or 0.25 inches.</li>
	<li>Code 128 codesets can be restricted to A, B, C or a combination of the three. Example: &quot;AB&quot; enables A and B. &quot;C&quot; just enables C and therefore only allows an even number of digits.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p><a name="N10061"></a><a name="Message+format"></a></p>

<h2>Message format</h2>

<div class="section">
<ul>
	<li>Valid characters: The full 7 bit ASCII (US-ASCII) character set is supported</li>
	<li>Functions 1 to 4 may be used. They are encoded as ASCII characters 0xF1 (241), 0xF2 (242), 0xF3 (243) and 0xF4 (244) in the message.</li>
	<li>Unlike with barcode fonts, the start, stop and checksum character shall NOT be part of the message as they are added automatically.</li>
</ul>
</div>
