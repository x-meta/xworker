<p><span style="background-color:rgb(238, 238, 238); color:rgb(0, 0, 0)">cc.RenderTexture is a generic rendering target. To render things into it,</span><br />
<span style="background-color:rgb(238, 238, 238); color:rgb(0, 0, 0)">simply construct a render target, call begin on it, call visit on any cocos</span><br />
<span style="background-color:rgb(238, 238, 238); color:rgb(0, 0, 0)">scenes or objects to render them, and call end. For convenience, render texture</span><br />
<span style="background-color:rgb(238, 238, 238); color:rgb(0, 0, 0)">adds a sprite as it&#39;s display child with the results, so you can simply add</span><br />
<span style="background-color:rgb(238, 238, 238); color:rgb(0, 0, 0)">the render texture to your scene and treat it like any other CocosNode.</span><br />
<span style="background-color:rgb(238, 238, 238); color:rgb(0, 0, 0)">There are also functions for saving the render texture to disk in PNG or JPG format.</span></p>
