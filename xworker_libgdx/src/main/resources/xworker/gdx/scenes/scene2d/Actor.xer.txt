<div class="block">2D scene graph node. An actor has a position, rectangular size, origin, scale, rotation, Z index, and color. The position corresponds to the unrotated, unscaled bottom left corner of the actor. The position is relative to the actor&#39;s parent. The origin is relative to the position and is used for scale and rotation.
<p>An actor has a list of in-progress <a href="http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/nightlies/docs/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/Action.html" title="class in com.badlogic.gdx.scenes.scene2d"><code>actions</code></a> that are applied to the actor (over time). These are generally used to change the presentation of the actor (moving it, resizing it, etc). See <a href="http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/nightlies/docs/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/Actor.html#act(float)"><code>act(float)</code></a> and <a href="http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/nightlies/docs/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/Action.html" title="class in com.badlogic.gdx.scenes.scene2d"><code>Action</code></a>.</p>

<p>An actor has two kinds of listeners associated with it: &quot;capture&quot; and regular. The listeners are notified of events the actor or its children receive. The capture listeners are designed to allow a parent or container actor to hide events from child actors. The regular listeners are designed to allow an actor to respond to events that have been delivered. See <a href="http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/nightlies/docs/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/Actor.html#fire(com.badlogic.gdx.scenes.scene2d.Event)"><code>fire(com.badlogic.gdx.scenes.scene2d.Event)</code></a> for more details.</p>

<p>An <a href="http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/nightlies/docs/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/InputListener.html" title="class in com.badlogic.gdx.scenes.scene2d"><code>InputListener</code></a> can receive all the basic input events, and more complex listeners (like <a href="http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/nightlies/docs/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/utils/ClickListener.html" title="class in com.badlogic.gdx.scenes.scene2d.utils"><code>ClickListener</code></a> and <a href="http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/nightlies/docs/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/utils/ActorGestureListener.html" title="class in com.badlogic.gdx.scenes.scene2d.utils"><code>ActorGestureListener</code></a>) can listen for and combine primitive events and recognize complex interactions like multi-click or pinch.</p>
</div>
