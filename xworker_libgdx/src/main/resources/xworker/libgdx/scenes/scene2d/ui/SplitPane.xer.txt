#$@text#$@
<p>A container that contains two widgets and is divided either horizontally or vertically. The user may resize the widgets. The child widgets are always sized to fill their half of the splitpane.</p>

<p>The preferred size of a splitpane is that of the child widgets and the size of the <a href="file:///E:/资料/编程/libgdx/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/ui/SplitPane.SplitPaneStyle.html#handle"><code>SplitPane.SplitPaneStyle.handle</code></a>. The widgets are sized depending on the splitpane&#39;s size and the <a href="file:///E:/资料/编程/libgdx/api/com/badlogic/gdx/scenes/scene2d/ui/SplitPane.html#setSplitAmount(float)"><code>split position</code></a>.</p>

<p><strong>子角色的设置：</strong></p>

	如设置firstWidget和secondWidget。<br />
	&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>其次通过添加相应的子节点</strong><br />
	属性未添加角色时，才通过FirstWidget和SecondWidget子节点创建角色。</li>
</ul>
