<p style="text-align: left;"><a href="http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/nightlies/docs/api/com/badlogic/gdx/utils/Scaling.html#none" style="color: blue; text-decoration: none;"><code>Scaling.none</code></a> keeps the aspect ratio by using a fixed size world (the world may not fill the screen or some of the world may be off screen).</p>
