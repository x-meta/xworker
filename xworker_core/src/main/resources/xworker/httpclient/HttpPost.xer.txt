<p>通过HttpPost请求一个uri。</p>

<p><strong>可以重写的方法：</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li><strong>getHeaders</strong><br />
	返回要设置的Headers。<br />
	&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>getHttpParams</strong><br />
	返回要设置的HttpParams。已废弃。<br />
	&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>getHttpClient</strong><br />
	返回HttpClient。<br />
	&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>getUri</strong><br />
	返回要请求的uri。<br />
	&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>getHttpEntity</strong><br />
	返回要Post的Entity对象，默认通过entity子节点设置。</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>事件：</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li><strong>onSuccess(HttpClient httpClient, HttpRequest request, HttpResponse response, HttpEntity entity)</strong><br />
	当HttpPost请求成功后触发，参数httpClient，request,response,entity，具体类型见Apache的HttpClient包。<br />
	默认返回请求返回的字符串。<br />
	&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>onException(HttpClient httpClient, HttpUriRequest request);</strong><br />
	当HttpPost请求发生Java异常时调用，参数httpClient，request, exception.<br />
	默认返回exception。</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>返回值: </strong>返回onSuccess或onException的返回值，其中如果onException返回的是Exception对象，那么抛出异常。</p>
