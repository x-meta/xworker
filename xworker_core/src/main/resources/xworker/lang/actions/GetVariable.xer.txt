	&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>varOgnlExpression</strong><br />

	&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>thingPath和thingActionName</strong><br />
	如果不为空，执行thingPath = parentSelf.getString(self.getString(varName)),然后获得thing，如果thingActionName不为空执行动作，否则返回thing。</li>
</ul>
